# UsbNotifier
Play under Windows with Usb Notifier device 1294:1320

## Program usage

  >UsbNotifier.exe [0-7]
  
Switch on/off LED with binary address:

LED    |  1  |  2  |  3  | Decimal
------ | ----|-----|-----|-------
Status | On  | Off | Off | 1
Status | Off | On  | Off | 2
Status | On  | On  | Off | 3
Status | Off | Off | On  | 4
Status | On  | Off | On  | 5
Status | Off | On  | On  | 6
Status | On  | On  | On  | 7
